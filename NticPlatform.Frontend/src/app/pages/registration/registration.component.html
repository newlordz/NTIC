Privacy Policy

NTIC Ghana - Data Privacy Notice

  1. Data Controller: National Technology & Innovation Championship (NTIC) Ghana, operating under the Ministry of Education.
  2. Purpose: Personal data is collected solely for championship registration, communication, certification, and statutory reporting to the Ministry of Education and Ghana Education Service.
  3. Legal Basis: Processing is based on consent (this registration), contractual necessity (participation agreement), and legal obligation (Act 843).
  4. Data Collected: Name, contact details, institutional affiliation, date of birth, guardian info, academic records, and competition submissions.
  5. Retention: Data is retained for 3 years post-championship for certification and alumni tracking, then anonymized or deleted per Act 843.
  6. Sharing: Data is shared only with authorized NTIC staff, competition judges, and the Ministry of Education. No third-party marketing.
  7. Rights: You may request access, rectification, erasure, or portability of your data by contacting privacy@ntic.org.gh.
  8. Security: Data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256) on Ghana-hosted servers.
  9. Cookies: This platform uses essential session cookies only. No tracking or analytics cookies.

By continuing, you acknowledge this privacy notice.
